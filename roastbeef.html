Mushroom Slow Cooker Roast Beef

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 1 (4 pound) standing beef rib roast
  • 1 (1.25 ounce) envelope onion soup mix
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) bottle beer
  • ground black pepper

    • Directions

    1. Place the mushrooms in the bottom of a slow cooker
    2. set the roast atop the mushrooms
    3. sprinkle the onion soup mix over the beef and pour the beer over everything
    4. season with black pepper. Set slow cooker to LOW
    5. cook 9 to 10 hours until the meat is easily pulled apart with a fork.
